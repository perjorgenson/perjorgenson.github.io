Pisco Sour At Home

July 27, 2025

A protein rich twist on the classic Pisco Sour. This one is dedicated to Kelcey, even though he’d hate this recipe in particular. The “At Home” part is homage to the fact that I do not own a cocktail shaker.

Ingredients and Tools:

  • 1 whole egg
  • Half a lime, and a way to juice it
  • A few dashes of aromatic bitters
  • 2 ounce pisco
  • 1 ounce simple syrup
  • Some ice cubes
  • A blender bottle (without the metal whisk)
  • Your favorite drinking vessel

Steps

  1. Put the ice cubes into the blender bottle first, so you don’t splash yourself with the mixture later
  2. Add the pisco, simple syrup, lime juice, and ice cubes to the blender bottle
  3. Crack the egg, and carefully separate the yolk from the whites
  4. Slonk the egg yolk (this is the twist)
  5. Put the cap on the blender bottle and shake-shake-shake!
  6. Pour into your favorite drinking vessel
  7. Give the bitters a shake-shake onto the foam on top of the drink

It’s up to you whether or not to include the ice cubes in your glass.
